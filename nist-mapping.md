# NIST Mapping Summary

This project aligns with NIST cybersecurity standards.

## NIST CSF Mapping
- Identify: Asset inventory and classification
- Protect: Access control, MFA, patching
- Detect: Risk scenario identification
- Respond: Risk mitigation planning
- Recover: Backup and recovery planning

## NIST RMF Alignment
- Categorize: Identify critical assets
- Assess: Evaluate risk likelihood and impact
- Respond: Recommend mitigation controls
- Monitor: Improve controls over time
